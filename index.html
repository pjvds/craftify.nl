About Me

My name is Pieter Joost van de Sande and I am a software development craftsman with an impressive track record that can help you, your team and project to accelerate.

I regularly present at conferences like Microsoft TechDays, SDC and NDC. I'm a one of the founders of Devnology where I help to organise meetings and run a podcast. My role in the community is recognised by Microsoft who awarded me with the exclusive Microsoft MVP award.

I contribute to many open source projects. As you can see at my Github profile, the contributions are not bound to a specific programming language or platform. I like to explore different languages and paradigms. I count C# as one of worlds most beautiful programming languages, and I am in love with Google's Go. I prefer staticly typed languages in most situations, but you also find me writing Javascript, Python, some Ruby and Smalltalk.

To clear my head and recharge my batteries I run barefoot.
